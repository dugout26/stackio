HOW TO PLAY

Movement

Desktop: Move mouse to aim, character follows cursor.

Mobile: Touch and drag the virtual joystick.

Combat

Weapons fire automatically at nearby enemies!

You start with Orbit + Bullet. Level up to unlock more.

Level Up

Collect XP orbs from killed enemies to level up.

Choose from 3 options: new weapons, upgrades, or passives.

Weapon Evolution

Max out 2 compatible weapons (Lv.5) to unlock a powerful evolved weapon!

Orbit + Shockwave = Plasma Storm

Bullet + Laser = Railgun

Laser + Orbit = Death Ray

Bullet + Shockwave = Meteor Shower

Mines + Shield = Fortress

Mines + Shockwave = Void Traps

Tips

Stay in the safe zone (green circle) when you spawn.

Kill pentagon mobs for the most XP!

Build toward weapon evolutions for maximum power.
